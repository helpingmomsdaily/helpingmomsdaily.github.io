Sleeping

Pets

Keep pets out of the room (especially cats!)

Crib

The crib should be empty (no soft pillows, blankets, toys, etc)

The sleep surface should be firm and covered with a fitted blanket

Baby

Place the baby on their back every time

Keep their head and face uncovered

Dress them in sleep clothing such as a one-piece or wearable blanket

Pacifiers can be offered (not forced) when putting down to sleep as long as the baby has been breastfeeding for about a month and the breastfeeding is going well

"Rooming In"

This is where the parents sleep in the same room as the baby.

Babies cry less and get fed more often

Parents get more peace of mind, rest, and confidence
