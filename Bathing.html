Bathing

Check with your doctor and pediatrician first to make sure it is OK to start giving your baby a bath!

Supplies needed:

  • mild soap
  • wash cloth
  • plastic cup
  • towel
  • diaper
  • clothes

Temperature

  • Keep the water temperature comfortably warm and keep the room warm too.

What to Wash Them In:

  • You can give your baby a bath in a baby tub or a sink.

Washing the Baby:

  • Wash the babyâ€™s face, body, and diaper area (in that order).
  • Make sure to get all folds and creases (such as under the neck).
  • Rinse the baby by filling the plastic cup with warm water and pouring it over them to remove the soap.

Frequency:

  • Bathe babies around 3 times a week at the max.
  • Wash their face, hands, and diaper area in between the bath times.
