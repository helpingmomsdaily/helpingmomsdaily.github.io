Behavior

Common Reasons for Crying

  • Baby is hungry or wants to suck
  • Baby needs a diaper change
  • Baby wants to be held
  • Baby is hot or cold
  • Baby is lonely

Colic

Babies may get colic and it can cause them to have many long periods of crying or fussiness. In the case of colic, there can be times that there is little you can do to comfort your baby.
