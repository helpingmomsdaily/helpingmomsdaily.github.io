Healthy Lifestyle

Exercise

Try to exercise!

It can help you feel better mentally/physically and help you recover.

It can help you sleep better, relieve stress, and prevent depression

Good exercises include walking and exercises to strengthen your back and stomach muscles

Nutrition

  • Drink lots of water
  • Try to eat at least 3 balanced meals a day
  • Look for foods that are high in calcium, Vitamin D, folic acid, and protein
  • Eat a variety of protein, carbs, and fats
  • Eat foods that are high in fiber
  • Eat small snacks throughout the day
