"Blues"

Many parents experience negative feelings for a few weeks after birth

Common Symptoms Include: impatience, irritability, restlessness, anxiety, feeling tired, insomnia, sadness, mood changes, and poor concentration

Some Helpful Activities: Get plenty of rest, get out in nature, move around (walk, dance, etc), do things you love, make time for yourself
